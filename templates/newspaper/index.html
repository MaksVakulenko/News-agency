News Agency Management System

Project Overview

This system helps newspaper agencies track and manage their publications and editorial staff. Key features include:

Core Features:

  • Track newspapers with titles, content, and publication dates
  • Manage editors (redactors) and their experience levels
  • Organize content by topics
  • Associate multiple editors with each newspaper
  • Assign multiple topics to newspapers

System Components:

1. Newspapers

Manage publications with detailed information including:

  • Title and content
  • Publication date
  • Associated topics
  • Assigned editors

2. Editors (Redactors)

Track editorial staff information:

  • Personal details
  • Years of experience
  • Published newspapers

3. Topics

Categorize newspapers by topics for better organization
